Our Story

Diana and Won spend most of their time playing games, watching anime, and hanging out with their two loving cats, Mowgli and Couch. They've found each other's permanent player 2 and look forward to exploring the world (both real and virtual) in the next chapter of their lives.

July 2014

Wongi lands in California, right after college, far from home.

July 2015

Diana comes to California to start work and starts hanging out with Wongi.

October 2015

After hanging out a lot, Diana and Wongi start dating.

August 2016

They start playing their primary game of choice together (World of Warcraft) for Legion prepatch!

April 2017

Diana convinces Wongi that cats are better than dogs and they adopt Mowgli (from craigslist) and kept him hidden in Diana's apartment (no pets allowed!).

August 2017

Wongi starts law school at Davis and lives in Sacramento but drives back to SF every weekend to spend time with Diana.

January 2018

Wongi misses Diana too much so he starts commuting from SF to Davis every day.

May 2018

They get their first cutting edge! Wongi proudly claims to be top 3 warrior (on the server). Here they are at Blizzcon:

January 2018

Diana and Wongi move to a tiny studio in SF and live together with Mowgli. Wongi continues to commute to Davis to finish law school.

December 2018

Diana and Wongi move to Walnut Creek, the perfect midpoint between Diana's job and Won's law school.

Fall/Winter 2020

Wongi graduates law school, passes the bar, and starts his firm job! They commemorate Wongi's farewell to free time with a commission of their WoW characters.

November 2019

Adopted Couch. They tell people that it was so Mowgli would have a brother but really they just wanted a second cat.

2021/2022

After supporting Wongi through law school, it's Diana's turn to take a break :) She is funemployed for over a year, pursuing hobbies like crafting and laying down.

December 2023

Wongi proposes to Diana by the bay and she says yes! They spend that night at the ER for 6 hours after Diana has some bad oysters…

December 2024

After being engaged for a full year (with zero movement on the wedding) Diana decides it'd be best to hire a planner.
Thanks Maggie!

2025 and onward

Diana and Wongi are still managing to find new ways to enjoy each other's company 10 years later.
